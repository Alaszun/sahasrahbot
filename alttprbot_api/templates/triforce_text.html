Triforce Text Submission

This is a form for submitting text to the Triforce. All submissions are subject to review and approval by the tournament staff. Submissions that may be rejected include, but are not limited to:

  1. Real-life religion or politics
  2. Hate speech
  3. Advertising (unless previously approved)
  4. References to other community members without their prior consent (will be verified with that person)
  5. Harassing or other overtly negative content
  6. Text inappropriate for streaming on SpeedGaming, ALTTPRandomizer, or other Twitch channels.

In short, keep it fun and positive!

Each submission must be no more than three lines, with a maximum of 19 characters per line.

Valid characters include:

  • A-Z
  • a-z
  • 0-9
  • spaces
  • ? ! , \ - … . ~ ～ ' ' ↑ ↓ → ←
